My name is Xander Koo (he/they).

I'm a Ph.D. student in Human-Centered Computing in the School of Interactive Computing at Georgia Tech, advised by Dr. Amy Bruckman. My research interests lie at the intersection of social computing, labor, and politics.

I graduated from Pomona College (chirp chirp!) in 2021, where I majored in Computer Science & minored in Asian Studies.

Outside of tech, I'm interested in street/hip-hop dance, Taiwanese & Japanese culture, and any intersections thereof. I also love cooking!
