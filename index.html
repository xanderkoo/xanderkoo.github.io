hi, i'm xander

My name is Xander Koo (he/they). I'm a senior at Pomona College, graduating Dec 2021, with a major in computer science & a minor in Asian studies.

This year, GuideBand, a research project that I worked on at National Taiwan University with Dr. Hsin-Ruey Tsai was accepted to CHI 2021 !!!

At Pomona, I work as an RA on a project called WebGazer, a browser-based webcam gaze prediction system, with Prof. Alexandra Papoutsaki.

This past summer I was a remote intern on the Key Protect software development team at IBM.

In Fall 2020, I was on a semester-long leave of absence, working as a remote Visiting RA at the Portal-ble HCI Lab @ Brown University. I worked on integrating MediaPipe hand tracking in a project called Portal-ble.

(I made this website from scratch! The background was made in p5.js.)

about me

I'm broadly interested in HCI -- social computing, I'd also love to explore topics related to computer vision, AI/ML, or tech art.

Outside of CS, I'm interested in tech ethics, activism, street/hip-hop dance, Taiwanese & Japanese culture, and any intersections thereof.

Recently, I've been practicing how to cook and play the guitar. I'm also a huge street dance nerd, so I love learning about cool stuff related to breakin' and street dance in general.
