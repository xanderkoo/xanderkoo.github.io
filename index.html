hi, i'm xander koo

My name is Xander (he/they). I'm a senior(-ish) at Pomona College graduating Dec 2021, with a major in computer science & a minor in Asian studies.

At Pomona, I work as an RA on a project called WebGazer, a browser-based webcam gaze prediction system, with Prof. Alexandra Papoutsaki.

Last Fall, I was on a semester-long leave of absence, working as a remote RA at the HCI Lab @ Brown University. I worked on integrating MediaPipe hand tracking in a project called Portal-ble.

(I made this website from scratch! The background was made in p5.js.)

about me

I'm broadly interested in HCI, AI/ML, and computer vision. I'd also love to explore topics related to NLP, graphics, or tech art.

Outside of CS, I'm interested in tech ethics, political activism, street/hip-hop dance, Taiwanese/Japanese culture, and any intersections thereof.
